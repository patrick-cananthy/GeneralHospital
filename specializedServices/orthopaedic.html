Orthopaedic

The orthopedic clinic at Tema General Hospital is dedicated to diagnosing and treating a wide range of musculoskeletal conditions. They offer specialized care for issues related to bones, joints, muscles, and connective tissues. Services typically include consultations, diagnostic imaging (such as X-rays and MRI scans), non-surgical treatments (like medication and physiotherapy), and surgical interventions when necessary. The clinic is staffed by orthopedic specialists who are trained to provide comprehensive care tailored to each patient's needs, aiming to improve mobility, alleviate pain, and enhance overall quality of life.
